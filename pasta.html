Pasta

A quick and easy pasta recipe.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  • Boil pasta until al dente.
  • Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  • Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  • Mix pasta with sauce.
  • Top with Parmesan and serve.
