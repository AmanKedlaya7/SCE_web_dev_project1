Tacos

Delicious and easy-to-make tacos.

Ingredients

  • 4 taco shells
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Lettuce, chopped
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Salsa

Steps

  • Cook ground beef in olive oil.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Warm taco shells.
  • Fill shells with beef, lettuce, and cheese.
  • Top with salsa and serve.
