Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • Butter for cooking

Steps

  • Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  • Add milk and egg, mix until smooth.
  • Heat butter in a pan.
  • Pour batter and cook until bubbles form.
  • Flip and cook until golden. Serve warm.
